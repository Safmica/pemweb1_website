Hogwarts Trophy Achievement

The House Cup

The winner of Houses was Hufflepuff, crowned after a series of exciting trivia battles. The winning trio, consisting of David Martin, Luke Neville, and Adriana Redding, showcased their house traits of loyalty and patience to overcome competitors from Ravenclaw and Slytherin in the grand finale.

Triwizard Tournament Trophy

The winner of the Triwizard Tournament in the 1994-1995 competition was Harry Potter and Cedric Diggory, who both reached the Triwizard Cup simultaneously in the final task.

The Quidditch Cup

Gryffindor, led by Captain Oliver Wood, won the Hogwarts Quidditch Cup in 1994, 1996, and 1997, showcasing the competitive spirit and talent of its student teams. Harry Potter's exceptional skills as Seeker and Ginny Weasley contributed to their success.

About Hogwarts

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is a renowned, magical boarding school that has been shaping the brightest witches and wizards for centuries. Founded over a thousand years ago, Hogwarts provides a transformative education for young magic users.


With a focus on holistic growth, Hogwarts nurtures each student's magical abilities while encouraging bravery, intelligence, loyalty, and ambition through its four iconic houses: Gryffindor, Ravenclaw, Hufflepuff, and Slytherin.
